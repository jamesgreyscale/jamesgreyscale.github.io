ListFast Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

ListFast is built so that we cannot access your data, because it never leaves your device.

What we collect

Nothing. ListFast has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs. Your items, item photos, form details, and the listing copy the app composes are stored only in the app's local database on your device.

Camera and photos

The camera is used solely to photograph the items you want to list. Photos you take or import are stored inside the app on your device. They are never uploaded, analyzed remotely, or shared with anyone — including us.

How listing copy is generated

Listing titles, bullets, condition paragraphs, and shipping blocks are composed entirely on your device from the details you enter. Nothing you type or photograph is sent to any server or AI service. ListFast is not affiliated with eBay, Poshmark, Mercari, or any marketplace.

What leaves your device

  • Nothing automatically. The app makes no network requests of its own.
  • Purchases: the optional Pro subscription and listing-credit packs are processed entirely by Apple. We never see payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies: apple.com/legal/privacy.
  • Listings you export or copy: the listing text and packs you generate go wherever you paste or send them. That's under your control.

Data retention & deletion

Your data lives in the app. Deleting an item deletes its records and photos. Deleting the app deletes everything — export or copy your listings first if you want to keep them. Purchased listing credits are stored on your device and are not restorable across devices.

Children

ListFast does not collect data from anyone, including children, and contains no ads.

Changes

Any future changes will be posted at this URL and noted in release notes.
