Odolog Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026

Odolog is built so that we cannot access your data, because it never leaves your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Odolog has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs. Everything you enter — vehicles, service records, fuel entries, reminders — is stored only in the app's local database on your device.

What leaves your device

  • Nothing automatically. The app makes no network requests of its own.
  • Purchases: the optional Odolog Pro unlock is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to the transaction: apple.com/legal/privacy.
  • Exports you initiate: if you export a CSV or backup file, it goes wherever you send it (Files, AirDrop, email, etc.). That's under your control.

Notifications

Maintenance reminders use local notifications scheduled on your device. No push servers are involved.

Data retention & deletion

Your data lives in the app. Deleting a vehicle deletes its records. Deleting the app deletes everything (make a backup first — Settings → Back up everything).

Children

Odolog does not collect data from anyone, including children, and contains no ads.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be posted at this URL and noted in the app's release notes.

Contact

Questions: support@8greyscale.com
