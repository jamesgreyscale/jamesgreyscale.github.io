Hitchlist Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026

Hitchlist is built so that we cannot access your data, because it never leaves your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Hitchlist has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs. Your checklists are stored only in the app's local database on your device. The app requests no permissions at all — no location, no notifications, no camera.

What leaves your device

  • Nothing automatically. The app makes no network requests of its own.
  • Purchases: the optional Pro unlock is processed entirely by Apple. We never see payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies: apple.com/legal/privacy.
  • Exports you initiate: PDF checklists go wherever you send them.

Data retention & deletion

Your checklists live in the app. Deleting a checklist deletes it; deleting the app deletes everything. Print or save a PDF first if you want a keepsake copy.

A note on checklist content

Bundled templates are generic starting points contributed from common RVer practice. They are reminders, not instructions — always follow your rig's owner's manual and your own judgment.

Children

Hitchlist does not collect data from anyone, including children, and contains no ads.

Any future changes will be posted at this URL and noted in release notes.
