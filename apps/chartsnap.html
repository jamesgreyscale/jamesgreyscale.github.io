ChartSnap

Read any chart in seconds — as a probability, never a promise.

Point your camera at any trading chart, or upload a screenshot, and ChartSnap scores it across six independent confluence factors — then shows an educational BUY, SELL, or WAIT read with a confidence band, drawn right on top of your own chart.

  • Works on stocks, crypto, forex, futures, commodities, indices — any timeframe
  • Clear BUY / SELL / WAIT read with a confidence band, capped at 85% — markets are never certain
  • Suggested entry zone, stop loss, and two targets, drawn on your chart photo and shareable in a tap
  • Risk/reward checked automatically — setups under 1.5:1 are downgraded to WAIT
  • Six-factor confluence breakdown: trend structure, support & resistance, patterns, momentum, volume, context
  • No account, no API keys, nothing to configure

Probability, never a promise

There is no magic indicator that predicts markets, and anyone who says otherwise is selling something. ChartSnap takes the professional approach instead: multi-factor confluence scoring. A directional read only appears when several independent factors agree. When the picture is mixed, ChartSnap says WAIT — because most charts don't present a high-probability setup.

The honest Signal Scorecard

Most chart apps hide their track record. ChartSnap shows you yours. Mark how each signal played out — Hit TP1, Hit TP2, Stopped Out, or Didn't Take It — and the Scorecard reveals your real win rate overall, by confidence band, and by market. We show the numbers even when they're humbling, because the whole point is honesty: signals are probabilities, not certainties.

Your first three scans are free. Unlimited analyses come with an auto-renewing subscription (weekly or yearly), charged to your Apple ID and cancellable anytime in your Apple ID settings. ChartSnap is educational and does not promise any financial outcome.
